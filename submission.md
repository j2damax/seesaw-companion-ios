## §1 — Thesis Statement

> *A structurally privacy-preserving edge AI architecture can generate contextually relevant, interactive children's stories with zero PII transmission, at latencies comparable to cloud-dependent alternatives, with narrative quality ratings statistically indistinguishable from cloud-generated outputs.*

**Null hypothesis (H₀):**  
Story beats generated by the on-device architecture (Mode B — Apple Foundation Models) are rated statistically equivalent in quality to cloud-generated beats (Mode A — Gemini 2.0 Flash), while transmitting zero bytes of personally identifiable information.

**Statistical test used:** Kruskal-Wallis H-test (non-parametric, because generation time distributions are non-normal). H = 52.75, p < 0.001. Post-hoc Mann-Whitney U with Bonferroni correction: Apple FM vs Hybrid p = 0.59 (not significant); Gemma vs Hybrid p = 0.10 (not significant).

### Chapter 6 — Discussion

**Narrative arc to follow (from `SeeSaw-Project-Master.md` §11):**

1. **Privacy result:** All four modes transmitted `rawDataTransmitted = false` in 100% of sessions. Cloud and Hybrid send only anonymous ScenePayload labels (mean 1,120 bytes/request). Apple FM and Gemma send zero bytes. Structural privacy guarantee is upheld — not a policy claim but a verifiable architectural invariant.

2. **Latency result:** Apple FM achieves the fastest *end-to-end round-trip* (21.6 s) despite 69% slower raw generation than Cloud, because it produces 75% shorter story text. Cloud (28.3 s) and Gemma (29.9 s) have comparable round-trips. The on-device system is not slower than cloud alternatives when accounting for TTS duration.

3. **Quality result:** Parent-submitted ratings show hybrid mode achieves enjoyment 4.0/5, age-appropriateness 3.6/5, scene-grounding 4.0/5. Full rubric external-rater scoring pending (Step 13). Preliminary evidence does not contradict H₀ (on-device quality equivalent to cloud).

4. **Hardware constraint:** Gemma 3 1B requires 3 GB RAM — prohibitive for iPhone 12 (4 GB total RAM, 3 GB system overhead). Apple FM requires iOS 26+ but uses zero additional storage. Recommended minimum for Gemma deployment: iPhone 14+ (6 GB RAM).

5. **Novel VAD contribution:** Three-layer concurrent VAD (keyword + semantic + hard-cap) is a practical contribution to child-AI voice interaction design. Layer 2 semantic evaluation adds <2 s latency in 96% of cases while filtering incomplete child answers more reliably than a keyword-only approach.

## §5 — Statistical Results (Step 12)

Script: `scripts/analysis_step12.py`

**Kruskal-Wallis (generation time across 4 modes):**
- H = 52.75, p < 0.001
- Interpretation: At least one mode is significantly different. All modes differ from Cloud.

**Mann-Whitney U post-hoc (Bonferroni-corrected, α = 0.0083):**

| Pair | U statistic | p-value | Significant? |
|------|------------|---------|-------------|
| Cloud vs Apple FM | — | < 0.001 | ✅ Yes |
| Cloud vs Gemma | — | < 0.001 | ✅ Yes |
| Cloud vs Hybrid | — | < 0.001 | ✅ Yes |
| Apple FM vs Gemma | — | < 0.001 | ✅ Yes |
| Apple FM vs Hybrid | — | 0.59 | ❌ No |
| Gemma vs Hybrid | — | 0.10 | ❌ No |

**Key finding:** Apple FM and Hybrid are not statistically distinguishable in generation latency. This supports treating them as equivalent-latency architectures for the dissertation's quality comparison.
